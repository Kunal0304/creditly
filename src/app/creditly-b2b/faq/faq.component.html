Frequently Asked Questions

About Creditly

Is Creditly’s website approved and authorized by official authorities?

Creditly's platform is licensed by SAMA and has a selection of trusted banks and partners you can easily apply to for different banking products. You can have secure banking services and innovative financial solutions that are based on trust.

What is a credit card?

It is a card issued by a bank that authorizes the holder to obtain their needs of goods or services in the form of debt. The client is given a grace period for repayment with a credit limit and a maximum limit of use. You can review costs and terms associated with it to compare and apply for a card through Creditly’s platform.

How can I obtain a credit card?

You can apply for a credit card online via Creditly. After completing the registration process through which your information will be verified via official integrated channels, for example, but not limited to; National Information Center, Elm, and Public Pension, a list of credit card options from various banks and partners will be shown to you so you can compare, filter, and apply for your credit card.

How do I know which card suits me?

After registration, Creditly will give you a list of options from different banks so that you can compare and assess your needs to determine which card suits you the best.
